THE VAMPIRE DIARIES



SYNOPSIS

The Vampire Diaries is a supernatural television series. The story follows the life of Elena Gilbert, a teenage girl who lost both of her parents in a car crash. Eventually, she falls in love with Stefan Salvatore. A 162 year old vampire who she thinks is just a normal human being.

CHARACTERS
KATHERINE PIERCE
Katherine Pierce is a 500 year old vampire and the former lover of the two Salvatore brothers. She is the member of the Petrov family and she was born in Bulgaria around the 1400's.
STEFAN SALVATORE
Stefan Salvatore is the main male protagonist in the TV show. He was born in 1864 at the age of 17. From his dark past, he was known as "The Ripper." as he would continue to feed on human blood and kill multiple people. In the series, he meets Elena Gilbert (the main protagonist), a doppelganger of Katherine. Stefan develops a relationship with Elena yet they ended the relationship due to a competition with his brother, Damon Salvatore.
DAMON SALVATORE
Damon Salvatore was considered the antagonist at the start of the series. As he became a vampire, he turned evil when Katherine chose his brother over him. Following the seasons of the show, Damon falls in love with Elena Gilbert, whom he is in a romantic relationship with until the series finale.
